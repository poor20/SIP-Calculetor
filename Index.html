Why Use This SIP Calculator?

Investing in the US market requires discipline and foresight. A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a powerful tool for dollar-cost averaging, allowing investors to build wealth over time without timing the market. Whether you are planning for retirement (401k/IRA) or saving for a major purchase, understanding compound interest is key.

Our calculator provides an instant estimate of your potential returns based on monthly contributions and expected annual growth rates.
